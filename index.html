About

I am a graduate researcher focused on cross‑embodiment policy learning and robot vision. Leading the open‑source OXE++ initiative, I scaled 14 public datasets into 95 robot–dataset pairs (~7× growth) and pushed simulation success rates up to 67 %, with real‑world gains of 24–45 % and zero‑shot transfer up to 82 %.

Research
